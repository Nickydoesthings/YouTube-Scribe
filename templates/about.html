About YouTube Scribe

YouTube Scribe is a simple web application designed to help you quickly generate written summaries and tutorials from YouTube videos. Just enter the URL of a tutorial or DIY video, and YouTube Scribe will analyze the content and provide you with a detailed, easy-to-follow guide.

This tool leverages the power of Whisper for transcription and OpenAI for generating summaries, ensuring you get accurate and useful information every time.

Whether you're looking to save time or simply prefer written instructions, YouTube Scribe makes it easy to get the content you need from your favorite video tutorials.
